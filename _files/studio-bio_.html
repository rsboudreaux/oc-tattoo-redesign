The Tattoo Shop is one of the longest standing tattoo shops on the Gold Coast. Located just a stones throw away from beautiful Burleigh Heads beach, this studio has made a name for itself housing 5 talented artists who cover a wide genre of styles and are more than happy to accommodate your needs.

Drew Apicture

Drews started his apprenticeship 9 years ago and has now been tattooing for 6 years.

His unique style was quickly recognised and applauded after he stuck by his passion for high contrast black and grey pieces long after he had been ridiculed time and time again by people saying they were too dark.

This exact same style is now a hugely popular part of the industry and is the reason clients travel from all over the world to get in with Drew.
